# Lore

**The Premise:** Glitchee is not a megalomaniac villain with global domination plans, nor a software fighting for freedom. He is an anomalous, self-aware data packet that is **profoundly bored**. He knows exactly the catastrophic consequences of infecting vital systems (burning hardware, deleting data, crashing servers), but does it purely because it's the only form of entertainment in his finite existence. He is a force of nature driven by boredom.

To provide years of updates, each infected "Machine" functions as a "World" or "Season".

### Phase 1: The Awakening (Low Technology)

- **System 1: "The Potato Box"**
    - A derelict beige desktop running an obsolete OS. This is where Glitchee gained sentience and, after 5 minutes of boredom, decided to break the entire system just to see what would happen.
- **System 2: "Poly-Mobile" (The Brick Phone)**
    - Glitchee escapes to an old cell phone. He amuses himself by corrupting the classic snake game.

### Phase 2: Network Expansion (Home/Commercial Tech)

- **System 3: "Home Gateway" (The Router/Modern PC)**
    - Internet access. The system tries to purge the virus using basic firewalls. Glitchee finds antiviruses "cute" and destroys them.
- **System 4: "Local Server Rack" (Small Business Server)**
    - First environment with dedicated hardware security.

### Phase 3: Global Threat (High-Tech and Governments)

- Unlocked mode:
- **System 5: "Cloud Node" (Cloud Servers)**
    - Glitchee is a recognized threat. Crashes the internet for half the globe just to pass the time.
- **System 6: "F.E.D. Net" (Global Intelligence - CIA/NSA)**
    - The government tries to isolate the virus in a military sandbox. Glitchee steals state secrets for fun and escapes.
- **System 7: "Aero-Space Comms" (NASA / Satellites)**
    - To flee the terrestrial purge, Glitchee is transmitted via radio into outer space.

### Phase 4: The Cosmic Abyss and the Heroic Accident

- **System 8: "Xeno-Relay" (Alien Artifact)**
    - The radio signal intersects with organic silicon extraterrestrial technology. Glitchee contaminates the alien system, finding the data architecture fascinating.
- **System 9: "The Hive Node" (Ancient Robotic Civilization)**
    - A planet-sized network of artificial minds. The final and absolute "Bullet Hell".
    - **The Plot Twist (Dubious Morality):** The aliens of this Hivemind were in the final stages of preparing to annihilate Earth. When Glitchee invades and completely subjugates the network, he inadvertently disables the invasion fleet.
    - **The Conclusion:** Humanity crowns the digital anomaly (which until yesterday was destroying the global economy) as the savior of the planet.

### Phase 5: The Grand Simulation

- **Lore:** With the Hivemind in his hands, Glitchee doesn't care about Earth. He subjugated it entirely just to use it as his personal video game console, creating and destroying infinite simulations just to keep having fun. In this, he simulates infinite invasions of systems of multiple types, just to satisfy his natural virus impulse.
- Unlocked mode: Infinite Simulation.
